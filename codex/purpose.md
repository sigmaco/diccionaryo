# Term: purpose

## v1.0.0

**Definition**  
**Purpose** is the explicitly or implicitly defined reason for which something exists, is created, or is enacted. It is the **intended outcome**, **motivating aim**, or **directional function** embedded within any structure, action, object, or concept.
